About Me

I’m a full-stack software developer with 3 years of experience in the legal tech space, building and supporting scalable applications, APIs, and automation solutions used in production environments. I enjoy working across the stack and taking ownership of features from design through to delivery.

Before transitioning into software development, I spent 7 years working as a structural engineer. That experience shaped how I approach software today — with a strong emphasis on problem-solving, reliability, and clear, maintainable solutions.

My move into tech was driven by a desire to work closer to the systems that power modern products and to build efficient, data-driven tools that improve existing workflows. I particularly enjoy modernising platforms, collaborating across teams, and continuously learning as technologies evolve.

I care about maintainable code, thoughtful design decisions, and solving problems in a way that balances quality with practicality.

Outside of work, I enjoy team sports, staying active, travelling, and cooking simple, tasty dishes — all of which help keep me balanced and curious both professionally and personally.
